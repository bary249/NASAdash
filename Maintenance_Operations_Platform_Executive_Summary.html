03 ‚Äî Agentic Intelligence

AI That Acts, Not Just Reports

Our platform already runs AI-powered insights across every property. For Bozzuto, we extend this into autonomous maintenance agents that detect, decide, and act.

Agent 1

Turn Velocity Optimizer

Monitors every unit in the make-ready pipeline. When a turn falls behind SLA:

  • Identifies the bottleneck stage (e.g., Paint stuck 3 days)
  • Auto-pings the assigned vendor
  • Escalates to maintenance supervisor after 24h no-response
  • Recommends alternative vendor if SLA breach is imminent
Agent 2

Predictive Maintenance Scheduler

Uses historical work order data + asset age to predict failures before they happen:

  • HVAC replacement predictions based on unit age + service history
  • Auto-creates preventive WOs 30 days before expected failure
  • Seasonal prep batching (e.g., pre-winter boiler inspections)
  • Prioritizes by unit revenue impact (higher-rent units first)
Agent 3

Vacancy Revenue Recovery Agent

Ties maintenance directly to revenue impact:

  • Calculates real-time revenue loss per vacant day √ó market rent
  • Flags when turn delays exceed the cost of expedited service
  • Auto-generates "cost of delay" alerts for portfolio managers
  • Recommends pricing adjustments if unit sits vacant post-ready
Agent 4

Resident Churn Prevention Agent

Connects maintenance quality to resident retention:

  • Integrates with data warehouse risk scores for churn prediction
  • Correlates open WO age with churn likelihood
  • Auto-escalates WOs for high-risk-of-churn residents
  • Triggers proactive outreach when maintenance SLA fails
Agent 5

Review Sentiment Agent

Monitors online reputation across major review platforms:

  • NLP detects maintenance-related negative reviews in real-time
  • Maps complaints to specific unit/building/issue type
  • Auto-creates follow-up WOs from review complaints
  • Tracks response rate and sentiment trend over time
Agent 6

Due Diligence Audit Agent

Automates the acquisition audit workflow:

  • Ingests rent rolls from target properties via any supported PMS
  • Auto-flags lease anomalies (below-market rents, expired leases)
  • Physical walk app: offline photo capture + checklist per unit
  • Generates acquisition report with maintenance CapEx estimate
